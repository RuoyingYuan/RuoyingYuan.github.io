Tracking Microtubules

The data was provided by Ram Dixit @ WashU Biology


Geometric Computing for Biomedicine , 2022Fall

Microtubules are constantly growing/shrinking and even moving and spinning. Besides, the microscope video has different intensitive and contrasts in each frame. The tool we built allows the user to select a particular microtubule and the tool segment that microtubule in all frames of the video. The tool also records the growth of the target microtubule during the video.
